About

A small biography about myself that shows how i dived into the field of IT and what've done.

I'm 22 old full-stack web developer from Tunisia. My passion with code started years ago, almost 8 years now.

I carry both technical and academic knowledge. Currently I'm studying for my media engineering master's degree and I hold a bachelor degree in computer science. Despite my formal education, I made my self busy learning and working with versatile technologies. Throughout the years, I acquired the ability to solve problems and to adapt changes. Performance and usability are a priority for me, i always seek to write a suitable code for both humans and computers. As an innovator individual, I'm able to dive into new challenges and explore new ideas.

I created a variety of open source projects alongside to private projects in my freelancing career. I'm a kind of person who'd like to share knowledge and teach people what he knows which made me, with all humility, a reliable mentor for my university's colleagues. When we talk about teams I'm ready to be that strong link in the chain and be a part of technical decisions.

When it comes to my philosophy, I'm truly a simple man. I'd like going straight to the point. I try always to avoid the solutions or libraries that are bloated with unused or unnecessary codes, surprises are bad in this case. As, unexplored parts of code will make the developer in an out of control situation. We shouldn't let technology control us or used it out of context. For every problem there's a specific solution and I'm always thrilled to find that particular solution.
